 The beauty of making your own coffee-time 

Waking up early, commuting, working non-stop, eating with your colleagues, going back to working non-stop, getting home late, fixing a quick dinner, eating while watching videos on your cellphone if single or chatting with the family if you have one, and finally, hitting the pillow at night before doing it all again. Don't forget to meet your project deadlines, remember to pick up the kids after school, always take out the trash, better to do the laundry, plan your client-meetings, and mostly: don't be late.

Does this sound familiar to you?

Seems that something is missing, right?

For the sake of your internal peace of mind you need to take a pause. A special moment only for yourself, where you can be active and relaxed at the same time. How about making your beautiful coffee time, at home? And how about making your beautiful coffee time at home with cute and aesthetic accessories? Making your coffee at the privacy of your place is like a short mediation session. Making your beautiful coffee is like participating in a japanese tea ritual, where beautiful things can not be rushed to be done well.
