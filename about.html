 Our story 

Our journey started in 2019. Inspired by the asian trend of aesthetic making coffee, KAWAI KOFFEE came to light. The name of our company comes from the japanese word Kawaii, which means 'cute'. A small but cozy store was established in Barrie, where we provided personalized attention to our customers. Clients started to come interested in our original and cute accessories. Sales started to raise up, everything so peaceful and promissory

In 2020, COVID came to the world, and sadly we have to take a momentary pause in our business. Ironically, the lockdown gave our clients a boost to seek for relieve and relaxation through making beautiful coffee. Months later, we started to receive online orders and send them through courier only in the Barrie area, so that our first-generation clients would get their cute accessories.

After the end of the lockdown, KAWAI KOFFEE decide to keep working in the online store at the same time that working in the reopened physical store. Additionally, we expanded the range of delivery cities through all Ontario due the successful response we got. We are proud to say that our store is the number one in its category thanks to all your support.

We are still keeping only one store in Barrie, but there might be a second store in Toronto in the next year. Right now, we are a proud team of 12 people working to ensure you the best of our service. Our team has personal connections with teams from the same industry in South Korea so that our goods are high quality and original.
